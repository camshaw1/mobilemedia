Surveillance

The disappearance of disappearance

“The prospect of a world in which it becomes increasingly difficult to escape the proliferating technologies for data collection, storage, and sorting…”

Surveillance is everywhere yet nowhere, people are oblivious to what is right in front of them. This website aims to show you what can be collected about you through your own user input. A small series of interaction tests will take place and the data collected will be visually interpreted back to the user in a way to make them come to a realisation. This realisation is that companys moniter you online and collect and collate information about you and your online habits.
